Privacy Policy for Dumpix

Last updated: February 2026

Dumpix is a mobile application that allows users to manually browse, review, and delete photos stored on their device.

1. Photo Access

Dumpix requests access to photos stored on your device solely for the purpose of displaying them for manual review and deletion. All photo processing occurs locally on the user's device.

Dumpix does not upload, store, transmit, scan, analyze, or share any photos externally. No photo content leaves the user's device.

2. Data Collection

Dumpix does not collect, store, process, or share personal data.

The application does not require account creation and does not transmit user-generated content to external servers.

3. Advertising

Dumpix uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information for advertising and analytics purposes, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • Basic device information
  • Ad interaction data

This data collection is managed entirely by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Dumpix does not control or store this advertising data.

4. Data Security

Dumpix does not store personal data on external servers. All photo access remains local to the device.

5. User Control

Users may grant, deny, or revoke photo permissions at any time through their device settings.

Users may also reset or limit advertising identifiers through their device privacy settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Dumpix is not directed to children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
